Anything but cookie cutter.

Your space is a reflection of your individuality, your creativity, and your passions. That’s why every single one of our buildings start with a new plan to build unique, beautiful, liveable spaces that are each distinct. Letting the environment dictate the outcome, we focus on preserving heritage when we can, and using the bones of the structure to determine the look, feel, and function of every single one of our buildings.

Our residential and commercial buildings can be found predominantly throughout the city of Halifax, with a scattering of them around the rest of the province. Each space is full of life; a manifestation of modernity meeting history, a strong sense of originality, and an appreciation for beauty. Move away from the sameness. Move in to a Blackbay home.
